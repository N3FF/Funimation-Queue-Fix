Queue Reorder Fix

When turned on this will enable you to reorder your queue. The entire queue will be loaded, so if your queue is large it can take a while to load. You may want to disable after you are done reordering in order to lower queue load times.

Remove From Queue Fix

Sometime shows will show "Remove from Queue" on shows that are not currently in your queue preventing you from adding them. When this is turned on refresh the page, hold "shift", and move your mouse over the button you can now click "Add to Queue" and it will try and add the show instead of removing it.
